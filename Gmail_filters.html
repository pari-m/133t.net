Gmail Filters

Gmail’s filters can be used to filter email based on various parameters and apply actions on the filtered emails .
The filters can sort email based on sender,recipient, subject, keywords, presence of attachments and size of email.
Few of the actions that can be done are skipping the inbox, marking the email as read, starring email, applying a label, forwarding to an another address, deleting the email, sending canned responses

Feature availability

Gmail filters are available on all Gmail plans.

Creating a filter.

1.1. Goto Gmail filter settings and click on create new filter.

1.2. Add the criteria that you want to filter on
The various criteria are
• From - Filters based on who the email is from
• To - Filters email based on reciepients
• Subject - Filter by subject
• Has the words - Matches for words
• Doesn’t have - Looks for mail not containing words
• Has attachment - Filters emails that contain attachments
• Don’t include chats - Removes chats from the matches
• Size - Filters based on size of email

	Note: 
	• If multiple criteria are entered, only emails matching all the criteria are selected
	• Multiple inputs for the same criteria are allowed and boolean operators AND, OR can be used.	
	• Once you have selected the criteria, click on the create a filter with this search button

1.3. Gmail will now run the criteria against the email in your mailbox and list the emails that match the criteria. Go back to step 2 if you have to refine the criteria.

1.4. Go to applying actions to a filter

Applying actions to a filter

2.1 You have created a filter, The next step is to apply actions to the filter, the actions are what make Gmail’s filters so powerful.

2.2 Actions that are available in Gmail are for filtered messages are
• Skip the inbox - The emails will not go the inbox
• Mark as read - The emails will automatically get marked as read
• Star it - A star label will get applied to the emails
• Apply a label - The selected label will get applied to the emails
• Forward it to - This action frowards all matched emails
• Delete it - Delete all matched emails
• Never send it to spam - The mails will never be sent to spam
• Always mark as important - The mails will always be marked important
• Never mark as important - The mails will never be marked as important
• Categorise as - The mails automatically get categorised as the selected option

	Note: Multiple options can be selected.

2.3 Once you have selected all your options, click on create filter to create your filter.

2.4 Gmail will now prompt you if your filter is created successfully.
